Logical Schema (Entity with attributes, primary key, and foreign key)

1.  Passanger
    ---------
    Passenger_ID  PK
    Gender
    Customer_type
    Age
    Travel_type
    Satisfaction
    Booking_ID  FK - Online_Services.Booking_ID
    
    
2.  Flight
    ------
    Flight_ID  PK
    Class
    Seat_comfort
    Flight_distance    
    Cleanliness
    Dep_Arr_ID FK - Departure_Arrival.Dep_Arr_ID
    IS_ID FK - Services_Inflight.IS_ID
    
3.  Departure_Arrival
    -----------------
    Dep_Arr_ID  PK
    Convenience
    Departure_delay
    Arrival_delay
    
    
4.  Services_Inflight
    -----------------
    IS_ID  PK
    Wifi
    Entertainment
    Inflight_service
    Food_drink
    Onboard_service
    Legroom_service    
    
    
5.  Online_Services
    ---------------
    Booking_ID  PK
    Ease_of_online_booking
    Online_boarding   
    
    
6.  Airline_Services
    ----------------
    Airline_Service_ID  PK
    Checkin_service
    Gate_location
    Baggage_handling
    Passenger_ID FK - Passenger.Passenger_ID
